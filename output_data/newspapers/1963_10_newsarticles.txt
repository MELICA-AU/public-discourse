"1"	1182	1963	10	"Kriminelle uregelmæssigheder konstateret ved CF-regnskabet"	"Holbæk Amts Venstreblad"	"1963-10-15"	"At an extraordinary general meeting held at Vig Assembly Hall, it was revealed that criminal irregularities had been discovered in the accounts of the Civil Defense Federation in Nykøbing over the past few years. As a result, a court hearing on the matter will be held after the general meeting. Additionally, during the meeting, there was discussion about the introduction of a unified daily management for the civil defense work in Odsherred, including the appointment of a daily leader. Following discussions on the matter, it was decided to establish a committee to investigate the issues related to the potential hiring of a unified daily leader."	"Different opinions"
