"1"	1387	1971	7	"CF i Brønderslev har faaet en ny assistent"	"Vendsyssel Tidende"	"1971-7-4"	"The Civil Defense in Brønderslev has appointed a new civil defense assistant, Knud Jacobsen, who will assist Civil Defense Inspector Egon Hassing in coordinating efforts across the municipalities of Brønderslev, Løkken-Vrå, and Pandrup. The collaboration between the three municipalities aims to improve emergency planning and strengthen the effectiveness of the Civil Defense. Both Hassing and Jacobsen believe that the public has limited knowledge about Civil Defense, although Hassing is optimistic that awareness will improve in the next generation."	"Different opinions"
